THE LOAI
Northern Frontier Drama - Family Conflict - Winter Survival

BOI CANH
White Raven Homestead, vung Yukon nam 1894.

Mot can nha go biet lap giua rung thong phu tuyet. Ben ngoai la dem bao tuyet, gio rit qua vach go, tuyet dap vao cua so. Elias North, 68 tuoi, song cung chau trai Jonah, 15 tuoi, sau khi cha me Jonah qua doi. Elias it noi, nghiem khac, luon day Jonah cach song sot o vung bien gioi phuong Bac. Jonah thi ngay cang buc boi, muon roi khoi noi nay de theo mot doan xe hang xuong phia nam.

Bi mat trung tam: Elias khong cam Jonah di chi vi so hai. Nhieu nam truoc, cha Jonah cung bo nha trong mot dem bao va bien mat gan chinh dong song bang nay. Elias chua bao gio tim thay thi the con trai minh, va ong da giau Jonah su that do.

NHAN VAT
Elias North, 68 tuoi
Nguoi ong gia nua, mat nhieu nep nhan, doi mat sau va met moi. Ong cung ran, kiem che cam xuc, noi it nhung moi cau deu nang ne. Khi so hai, ong khong hoang loan lon tieng ma im lang, tho gap, mat rung len vi dang co giu binh tinh.

Mara Bell, 33 tuoi
Nguoi dua thu bang cho keo, ranh roi dia hinh, binh tinh va manh me. Co xuat hien nhu tia hy vong cuoi cung trong dem bao.
