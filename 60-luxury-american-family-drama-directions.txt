60 LUXURY AMERICAN FAMILY DRAMA DIRECTIONS

FICTIONALITY LOCK
Every story direction in this file is fictional. All families, scandals, buildings, estates, companies, schools, hotels, and private conflicts must be invented. Real American cities, neighborhoods, and states may be used only to create an authentic wealthy-American atmosphere. Do not imply any real person, real family, real case, real company scandal, or real crime.

NON-REPETITION LOCK
Do not reuse a direction by only changing names, cities, props, or family roles. Each selected direction must have a distinct dramatic mechanism, a distinct opening shock, a distinct relationship conflict, a distinct visible clue, and a distinct unresolved secret. If a new direction feels too close to an earlier one, discard it and create a different one.

SELECTION PURPOSE
Use this file as the first selection board only. Each direction is designed for a modern wealthy American city setting, with realistic fictional names, real-feeling upscale locations, family conflict, marriage tension, mother-in-law drama, neighborhood suspicion, theft, custody pressure, domestic intimidation, and high-society secrets.

1. THE PENTHOUSE ELEVATOR
Location: The Whitmore Residences, Upper East Side, New York City

Core Drama: Abusive husband, controlling mother-in-law, hidden surveillance video.
